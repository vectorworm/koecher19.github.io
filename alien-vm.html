Alien VM

how to play:
use WASD or ARROW KEYS to move
use SPACEBAR or RETURN to shoot
your remaining LIVE is indicated by the tank on the right

The general idea of this project is to construct a very dynamic alien shooter. The movement is controlled by a force system without dampening, enemies appear as swarms (see boids ). A random type of swarm spawns at given intervals. The gameplay is open ended. So the real goal is to get as far as you can.
